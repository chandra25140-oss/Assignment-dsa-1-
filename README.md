Find My Clothes

Problem Statement 1

Arranging Clothes in an Almirah

You have a wooden almirah with n shelves. The almirah is used to store your weekly wardrobe, which includes categories of clothing such as formal wear, casual wear, traditional attire etc.

Write an algorithm to arrange the clothes in the almirah while minimizing the time to find any item and ensuring optimum utilization of space.

Problem Statement 2

Arranging Clothes in a Luggage Bag

You are packing a single suitcase for a 7-day trip to a place/location of your hoice. You must pack clothes and accessories to suit daily activities, such as sightseeing, hiking, formal evening dinners etc.

Write an algorithm to pack the luggage bag while ensuring maximum utilization of space, easy accessibility of items, and protection of delicate clothing (if applicable).

2. Upload the two files (1.py and 2.py) to this assignment repository.
